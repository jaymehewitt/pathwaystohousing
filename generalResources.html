GENERAL RESOURCES


Volunteers of America

One of the nation's largest non-profit providers of affordable housing. Search for housing within the following categories: senior citizen, family housing, veterans housing, housing for persons with disabilities. Search for your local Volunteers of America office for additional services.


U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD)

Use the HUD Resource Locator to identify local affordable housing resources. Learn about the Housing Choice Voucher Program (section 8) for low-income families, the elderly, and the disabled, home-buying programs in your area, or search for an apartment. Find specific information for the disabled, veterans, and senior citizens.


HUD Homelessness Assistance Programs

Find contact information for organizations in your community that can assist people who are homeless or at risk of homelessness. Learn more about programs funded by HUD's Homelessness Assistance Program.


National Homeless Shelter Search

Find local homeless shelters and service organizations. Search for low-income and affordable housing.


Additional Shelter Search

Find local supportive housing and shelters, including homeless shelters, low-income housing, halfway housing, transitional housing, day shelters, and low-cost housing.


Homeless with Pets

Pets of the Homeless, a national animal organization focused on feeding and providing emergency care to pets of homeless people. Search for national and local resources, or resources by animal breed.
